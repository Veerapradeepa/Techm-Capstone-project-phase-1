Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my current balance? +

You can check your current balance in several ways:

  • Open the MobiComm app and view your balance on the home screen
  • Dial *123# from your registered mobile number
  • Log in to your account on the MobiComm website
  • Send a text message with the word "BALANCE" to 12345

Your balance information includes your remaining data, talk time, SMS, and account validity.

What happens if my balance runs out? +

When your prepaid balance runs out:

You won't be able to make outgoing calls or use data services. However, you can still receive incoming calls for a grace period of 15 days after your balance expires. You can still access the MobiComm app using WiFi to recharge your account.

To restore full service, simply recharge your account with any available payment method. Your services will be restored immediately after a successful recharge.

How do I activate international roaming? +

To activate international roaming on your MobiComm prepaid account:

  1. Open the MobiComm app and navigate to "Services"
  2. Select "International Services" and then "Roaming"
  3. Choose the countries you'll be visiting
  4. Select a roaming package that suits your needs
  5. Complete the payment to activate roaming

Note that you need to have sufficient balance in your account to purchase a roaming package. Activation usually takes 2-4 hours to process. We recommend activating roaming at least 24 hours before your travel date.

Can I transfer my balance to another MobiComm user? +

Yes, MobiComm allows balance transfers between prepaid users. To transfer balance:

  • Open the MobiComm app and go to "Services"
  • Select "Balance Transfer"
  • Enter the recipient's MobiComm number
  • Enter the amount you wish to transfer
  • Confirm with your PIN

There is a small service fee of 2% for each transfer. The minimum transfer amount is $5, and the maximum is $50 per day. Both accounts must be active and at least 90 days old to use this feature.

How do I report a lost or stolen phone? +

If your phone is lost or stolen, you should immediately:

  1. Use another phone to call our Customer Support at 1-800-MOBICOM to report the incident
  2. Our team will suspend your service to prevent unauthorized usage
  3. Log in to your MobiComm account from a different device and change your password
  4. File a police report if your phone was stolen

When you get a replacement phone, contact our support team to transfer your number to the new device. You may need to provide identification to verify you're the account owner.
